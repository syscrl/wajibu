Why Petitions Matter

Wajibu empowers every Kenyan to initiate change. Whether you're fighting for better roads, cleaner water, or equal rights—your voice, backed by your community, can push the government to act.

  • Community-Led Change

    When one person speaks, it's a whisper. When a community unites behind a petition, it's a roar that gets attention.

  • Trackable Civic Impact

    Every petition signed and shared is recorded. You’ll know what action was taken—and by whom.

  • Real Government Responses

    Top-voted petitions are sent directly to relevant officials, and we hold them accountable for their responses.
