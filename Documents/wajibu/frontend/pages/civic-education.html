Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about civic education and its importance.

1. What is Civic Education?

Civic education is the study of the rights and duties of citizens and how they can participate in governance.

2. Why is it important?

It empowers citizens to make informed decisions and hold leaders accountable.

3. How can I participate?

You can participate by voting, attending public forums, and engaging in community activities.

4. How does Wajibu help citizens?

Wajibu provides resources, tools, and platforms to educate and empower citizens.
