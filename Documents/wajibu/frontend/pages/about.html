About Wajibu

Empowering citizens. Promoting accountability. Enabling impact.

Who We Are

Wajibu is a civic-tech initiative that bridges the gap between citizens and the government in Kenya. We offer tools for civic engagement, accountability tracking, and civic education to promote transparent leadership and informed participation.

Our Mission

To empower every Kenyan with knowledge, tools, and data to hold leaders accountable, participate meaningfully in governance, and shape a more transparent society.

What We Offer

Scorecards

See real-time leader performance through verified scorecards and evaluation metrics.

Petitions

Start or support petitions on pressing community issues and push for real civic action.

Government Tracker

Track political promises, budgets, and development projects at county and national levels.

Civic Education

Access simplified, practical civic knowledge and know your constitutional rights.
